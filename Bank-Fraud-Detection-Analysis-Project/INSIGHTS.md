# BankSim Fraud Detection – Key Insights

1. Overview  
This dashboard analyzes synthetic banking transaction data to identify fraud patterns, high-risk categories, and vulnerable customer segments. The objective is to generate actionable insights to strengthen fraud detection and reduce financial risk.

2. Transaction Summary  
Total Transaction Value: ₹78.26 Lakhs  
Fraudulent Transaction Value: ₹37.88 Lakhs  
Total Transactions: 62,000  
Fraudulent Transactions: 7,145  
Fraud Rate: 11.55%  

A large share of the total transaction value is fraudulent, indicating significant financial exposure.

3. Category-Based Insights  
Travel category has the highest average fraud amount (~₹2,661), indicating high-value fraud cases.  
Sports & Toys and Health categories record the highest number of fraudulent transactions.  
Fraud is concentrated in specific categories rather than being evenly distributed.  

4. Customer Segment Insights  
Age group 26–35 shows the highest involvement in fraud, followed by 36–45.  
Working-age individuals are more active in fraudulent transactions.  

5. Gender-Based Insights  
Female users account for 66% of fraudulent transactions, compared to 34% for male users.  
This imbalance suggests possible targeted fraud patterns or behavioral trends.  

6. Merchant-Level Insights  
Fraud is concentrated among a small number of merchants.  
Top merchants contribute a disproportionately high share of fraud value.  

7. Risk Distribution Insights  
The fraud rate of 11.55% is relatively high.  
Fraud is clustered across specific categories, age groups, and merchants.  
This indicates that fraud detection mechanisms may not be fully effective.  

8. Key Takeaways  
Fraud is concentrated rather than random.  
High-value fraud exists in select categories such as Travel.  
A small group of merchants contributes heavily to fraud.  
Certain demographic segments are more vulnerable.  
Current fraud detection systems require improvement.  

9. Business Impact  
There is a high risk of financial loss due to concentrated fraud activity.  
There is a need for targeted monitoring based on category, user segment, and merchant behavior.  
The data presents an opportunity to implement more advanced and proactive fraud prevention strategies.
