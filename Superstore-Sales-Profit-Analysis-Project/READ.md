Superstore Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard

1. Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and business risks using the Superstore dataset.

The dashboard provides actionable insights to improve decision-making, optimize profit margins, and identify growth opportunities.

2. Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze business performance across different dimensions such as region, category, customer segments, and discount impact.

It helps in identifying loss-making areas, understanding customer behavior, and recommending strategic actions to improve overall profitability.

3. Key Metrics

Total Sales: $2.30M
Total Profit: $286K
Total Orders: 5,009
Profit Margin: 12%

4. Dashboard Features

Sales & Profit Overview
Provides a high-level summary of total sales, profit, and overall performance trends.

Regional Analysis
Analyzes sales and profit distribution across different regions and highlights top and loss-making states.

Discount Impact Analysis
Shows how different discount ranges affect sales and profitability.

Customer & Segment Analysis
Identifies top customers, segment contribution, and purchasing behavior.

Business Insights
Summarizes key findings from the data for quick understanding.

Loss Analysis
Highlights loss-making sub-categories, regions, and key reasons behind losses.

Profit & Growth Analysis
Focuses on high-performing categories and major profit drivers.

Customer Behavior Insights
Analyzes customer order frequency and retention opportunities.

Recommendations & Strategy
Provides actionable business strategies based on insights.

5. Key Insights

The business generated strong revenue but operates on relatively low profit margins.

Technology category is the main driver of both sales and profit.

Furniture category contributes to sales but suffers from low profitability.

West region performs best, while South and Central regions underperform.

Higher discounts significantly reduce profitability.

A small group of customers contributes a large portion of total sales.

6. Business Problems

Low profit margins despite high sales volume.

High discounts negatively impacting profitability.

Loss-making sub-categories such as Tables and Bookcases.

Regional imbalance in performance.

Limited repeat purchasing behavior from customers.

7. Recommendations

Reduce discounts above 20% to improve profit margins.

Focus on high-performing categories like Technology.

Improve profitability of Furniture category through pricing strategies.

Re-evaluate or discontinue loss-making products.

Improve performance in underperforming regions.

Introduce customer retention and loyalty programs.
